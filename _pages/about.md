I am a Master of Engineering student at [Southeast University (SEU)](https://www.seu.edu.cn/), Nanjing, China, expected to graduate in June 2027. My research focuses on the intersection of **Score-based Generative Models**, **Bayesian Inference**, and **Variational Methods** for solving high-dimensional inverse problems.

Currently, I am working on bridging diffusion priors with message passing algorithms under the supervision of [Prof. Xiaosi Tan](#) and [Prof. Chuan Zhang](#) at the [Lab of Efficient Architectures for Digital-communication and Signal-processing (LEADS)](#).

## Research Interests

- **Core Focus**: Score-based Generative Models for Inverse Problems, Bayesian Inference, Variational Methods
- **Methodologies**: Diffusion Models, Approximate Message Passing (AMP), Expectation Propagation (EP), Belief Propagation (BP)
- **Applications**: Compressed Sensing, High-Dimensional Signal Estimation, Iterative Receivers, Massive MIMO, Cell-Free MIMO
