First author. Proposed variational Bayesian message passing receivers for uplink integrated sensing and communication (ISAC) systems.

**Authors:** Tiancan Xia, Jian Zheng, Xiaosi Tan, Yongming Huang, Xiaohu You, Chuan Zhang

**Abstract:** This paper develops a message passing framework based on mean-field approximation for joint parameter estimation and signal detection in uplink ISAC systems. The framework is extended to Bethe variational free energy with noise variance updating equations derived via stationary point analysis. Convergence performance and estimation accuracy are analyzed through state evolution equations. The proposed method outperforms existing linear and interference cancellation-based baselines with comparable complexity.
