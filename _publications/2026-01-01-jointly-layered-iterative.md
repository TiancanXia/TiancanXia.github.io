First author. Proposed a jointly-layered iterative detection and decoding algorithm for coded massive MIMO systems.

**Authors:** Tiancan Xia, Xiaosi Tan, Yongming Huang, Xiaohu You, Chuan Zhang

**Abstract:** This paper introduces a novel architecture based on joint factor graphs enabling mini-batch information exchange between detection and decoding modules. The expectation propagation with successive updating (EP-SU) algorithm is restructured in a layered manner. A sorting and grouping strategy is designed to optimize the symbol update process. The proposed method demonstrates superior performance across various channel conditions compared to existing methods.
